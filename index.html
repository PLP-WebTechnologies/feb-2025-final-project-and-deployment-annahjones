About Me

Hello, I'm Ann Migwi, a poet, writer, and lover of words. My poetry is a reflection of my experiences, thoughts, and emotions. Through my words, I aim to connect with others and evoke feelings that resonate on a deeper level.

If you ever feel lost, undeserving, or unworthy, just seek within yourself the love that you give — it exists. Let’s make it till the end and create the best memories.

Most of my poems come from deep, raw emotions. I hope they inspire you to grow and know you aren't the only one going through a lot in life.

We all face ups and downs. I remind myself every day to give myself grace — to feel it all — because I was made to navigate this life, not perfectly, but fully.

My Poetry

Poetry has always been a passion of mine, serving as an outlet for self-expression and creativity. I write about a variety of topics — love, struggle, nature, dark emotions, and the human experience. Each poem is crafted with care, and I hope they touch your heart.

Whispers of the Night

In shadows deep where secrets lie,
The stars above begin to sigh.
My thoughts escape on midnight breeze,
And time stands still among the trees.

Echoes of Silence

Silence speaks in subtle ways,
Through golden dawns and misty haze.
I find myself in lines I write,
A poet’s soul in quiet light.
